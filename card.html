African Bush Elephant

African Elephants are the largest animals walking on Earth

  • Scientific Name: Loxodonta
  • Average Length: 13 Feet
  • Average Lifespan: 70 years
  • Habitat: Open Savanna

African Bush Elephants are the largest of 3 species of elephants. Their habitat spans from the open savanna to the dry desert. Adult elephants often reach up to 24 feet long and can grow up to 13 feet tall. Their weight can reach up to 11,000lbs. These animals are herbivores and eat mostly grass, bark, fruit, and a variety of leaves and foilage.
